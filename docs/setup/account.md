# Step 1: Register for a REDCap Account

This guide explains how to register for a REDCap account at the University of Oldenburg.

!!! tip "Official Information"
    For the most up-to-date information, visit the official REDCap page at [uol.de/fk6/fdm/redcap](https://uol.de/fk6/fdm/redcap).

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- [x] A valid University of Oldenburg account (university ID in format `abcd1234`)
- [x] Affiliation with Faculty VI (Medicine and Health Sciences)

## Registration Process

REDCap access at the University of Oldenburg is managed by the **Servicestelle Forschungsdatenmanagement (FDM)**.

### 1. Send a Request Email

Request access by sending an informal email to [fdm@uol.de](mailto:fdm@uol.de):

```
To: fdm@uol.de
Subject: REDCap Access Request

Dear FDM Team,

I would like to request access to the REDCap development environment.

My university ID: abcd1234
Purpose: [Brief description of your research project]

Thank you!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

!!! info "Development Environment First"
    You will initially receive access to the **development server** (redcapdev.uol.de) where you can test REDCap and decide if it's suitable for your project.

### 2. Wait for Activation

- The FDM team will process your request
- You will be activated as a user on the development environment
- You'll receive a confirmation email with login instructions

### 3. Access REDCap

Once activated, you can access:

| Environment | URL | Purpose |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| **Development** | [redcapdev.uol.de](https://redcapdev.uol.de) | Testing and learning |
| **Production** | [redcap.uol.de](https://redcap.uol.de) | Live data collection |

## Troubleshooting

??? question "I didn't receive a confirmation email"
    - Check your spam/junk folder
    - Wait 1-2 business days for processing
    - Send a follow-up email to [fdm@uol.de](mailto:fdm@uol.de)

??? question "I'm not affiliated with Faculty VI"
    - REDCap is primarily available for projects involving Faculty VI (Medicine and Health Sciences)
    - Contact the FDM team to discuss your specific case

??? question "I need help deciding if REDCap is right for my project"
    - The FDM team offers consultation services
    - Email [fdm@uol.de](mailto:fdm@uol.de) to schedule a meeting
